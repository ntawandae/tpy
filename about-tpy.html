WHAT DRIVES US

Who we are?

The precious you (TPY) is an organization which supports the equality and equity of men and women irrespective of age, race, religion, origin and cultural norms. We help equip and empower humanity across distinctions of society to help curb the effects of classes amongst ourselves. We provide motivational sessions, charity work, online mentoring, and online tutoring for students wherever you may be. Our focus is to make sure you do not feel inferior by giving you instruments to make a positive impact and identify you self-worth.

Our Values

Give hope, add smiles, add values, now being the new later make a difference today

Everybody should have freedom to develop their own identity, take pride in both their cultural heritage and personal accomplishments, and choose the best path of themselves.
